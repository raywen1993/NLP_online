Me and My Passion

My neme is Ruichen Wen. My passion is Natural Language Processing. I am a student of Master of Computing in Australian National University. I am doing my individual project right now, which is about Natural Language Processing (NLP). I am doing Relation extraction under few-shot learning, which means using just a small scale of training data to train a model properly. In lots of fields we don't have a large number of training data, and in this case we can use fine-tuning a pre-trained model to overcome over-fitting. The relation extraction is about figuring out the relationship between words and sentences, just like entailment recognisation.


MY GOAL: NLP online is designed to show the newest results in the Natural Language Processing (NLP) field. Our aiming group is those who are involved or just interested in NLP research. We will provide with some information about recent conferences, published articles and some tutorials designed for the beginners who are fond of NLP but have no background.
